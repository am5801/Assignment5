New York City's Resilience to Emergencies & Disasters Index

The proposed Resilience to Emergencies and Disasters Index (REDI) is a normalized score (1 - 100) for each Census tract's resilience capacity with respect to the City average. A score of 100 represents maximum resilience. Darker blue represents greater resilience capacity, while darker red represents greater vulnerability.
